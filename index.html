The Flood Crisis in Lower Atis

A Community's Struggle Against Rising Waters

Payatas B, Quezon City • Jomar A. Auditor

When the Waters Rose

In the early morning hours of June 5, 2024, residents of Lower Atis Street in Payatas B woke to find their homes rapidly filling with water. What began as typical monsoon rains quickly escalated into the worst flooding the community has seen in five years.

"

The water came so fast we barely had time to save important documents. Within 30 minutes, it was already waist-deep inside our house.

- Rosalinda Auditor, Residence of Lower Atis

Ambilis po umangat ng tubig, lagi po kami nagbabaha dito lapg kunting ulan lang baha agad. Sana po matulungan at maayos po ito.

- Emma, Residence of Lower Atis
